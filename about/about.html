¡Hola!

My name is Lizbeth Rodriguez and I am an aspiring Story Artist.
I was born and raised in Puerto Rico and I am currently living in Kissimmee, FL.
I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a BFA in Animation. I returned home to teach animation at summer camps and after-school programs. However, I had to put a stop in my career for a while to help take care of my family due to the events of Hurricane Maria.
In 2019, I moved to Florida in hopes of continuing my education and career aspirations. Currently, I am working as a Cast Member at Disney’s Animal Kingdom; and, through Disney Aspire, I am working on my Master in Mass Communication specializing in Web Design.
In my spare time, I am developing my skills in drawing and working towards a Storyboard Portfolio. I love drawing and over-analyzing stories from different media and genres. With my art, I hope to create stories that can bring hope and provide a new perspective in life.
